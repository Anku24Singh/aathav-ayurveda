What kind of Ayurvedic therapy services do you offer?

We offer a range of traditional Ayurvedic therapies including personalized consultations, herbal treatments, Panchakarma detoxification, Abhyanga (therapeutic massage), Shirodhara, and dietary/lifestyle counseling tailored to your unique body constitution (Dosha).

Do you offer online consultations or therapy sessions?

Yes, we provide online Ayurvedic consultations for those unable to visit in person. You can connect with our certified Ayurvedic doctors via video calls to receive personalized treatment and lifestyle guidance from the comfort of your home.

How can I schedule an appointment?

Appointments can be scheduled through our website, by calling our clinic directly, or by sending us a message on WhatsApp. We offer flexible timings and will confirm your slot via email or SMS.
